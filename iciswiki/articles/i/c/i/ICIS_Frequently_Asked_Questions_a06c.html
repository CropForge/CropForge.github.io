What does ICIS stand for?

International Crop Information System

What is ICIS?

It is a collection of databases (public and private), and associated query tools to help plant breeding programs with their bioinformatics needs by storing information related to the cultivars and data from field tests.

How can I get ICIS?

ICIS can be downloaded from here

How much does ICIS cost?

The International Crop Information System is an open-source project and is under the General Public License (GPL). Hence it is distributed free of charge.

Who uses ICIS?

http://cropwiki.irri.org/icis/index.php/ICIS_Users

Where can I get support?

Electronic support is available 24 hours a day, seven days a week. Using the cropforge , you can report new problems, ask questions, or supply additional information to previously tracked problems.

Who develops ICIS?

ICIS development is led by the IRRI-CIMMYT Crop Research Informatics Laboratory (CRIL) team, based in the Philippines and Mexico.
