Incoming Samples Composition (Report)

Overview

The Incoming Samples Composition form allows the user to generate a report summarizing the total number of incoming samples according to classification. There are four major classifications: 1) Cultivated Species, 2) Wild Species, 3) Related Genera, and 4) Inter-specific Hybrids/ Unidentified Species.
